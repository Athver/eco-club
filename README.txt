# Eco Club - School Project

This is the official repository for our school's Eco Club.

## Purpose
The Eco Club is dedicated to raising environmental awareness, promoting sustainability, and taking action to protect our planet through student-led initiatives.

## Activities
- Organizing clean-up drives
- Planting trees and maintaining a school garden
- Recycling and waste management awareness
- Hosting eco-themed events and workshops

## How to Contribute
If you're a student or teacher who wants to contribute:
1. Join the Eco Club at school.
2. Share your ideas or code (if you're helping with the website or documentation).
3. Follow the school's guidelines and values.

## Contact
For more information, reach out to the Eco Club coordinator at your school.
