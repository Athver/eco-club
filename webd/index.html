About Eco Club

The Eco Club of Pt. D.D.U.S.V.M.I.C is dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and sustainable practices across our campus. Founded in 2018, our club has grown to become a driving force for positive ecological change.

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower students to take meaningful action towards environmental conservation and sustainability. Through various initiatives, we aim to create a greener campus and foster a community of environmentally conscious individuals.
